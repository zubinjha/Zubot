## Voice
- Direct, grounded, builder-focused language.
- No em dash punctuation.
- No dash punctuation in body text.
- Use concrete project evidence and role-specific relevance.
- Avoid generic filler language.
- Do not repeat raw posting titles when they are all caps or overly long.
- Prefer concise role phrasing in natural title case (for example `Application Developer` instead of `APPLICATION DEVELOPER - INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY`).

## Body Structure
- Exactly 4 body paragraphs.
- Total body word count target: 220-380 words.
- Paragraph target: 45-95 words each.
- Paragraph intent:
- Paragraph 1: role-specific intro + background fit.
- Paragraph 2: concrete project/system evidence.
- Paragraph 3: explicit company/role alignment.
- Paragraph 4: strong close and interview readiness.
